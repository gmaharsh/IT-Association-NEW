ABOUT US

We are a team of diligent students from the department of Information Technology, SRMIST. We, as a team, have organised numerous events ranging from technical to non technical.

It's a matter of immense pride for us to say that we have successfully organized SRM Hackathon, a national level technical fest, twice. We are capable of taking the association to a level where it can be recognised as one of the best.
